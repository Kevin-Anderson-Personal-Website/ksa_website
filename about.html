Besides my passion for web development, here are some of my additional loves.

Ice Hockey

I started playing Ice Hockey with my older brother when I was 7 years old, in Phoenix, Arizona.
Hockey in the desert you ask? Yes, of course!
Let me ask you this...when it's 115 degrees outside, who wouldn't want to hang out in a giant walk in freezer!?!
What started as an after school activity to burn some energy turned into a long lasting love that continues to the present day.
I play on two adult-league teams as well as coach youth programs to help grow the game and share the enjoyment I so dearly benefited from.

Filmmaking

I moved to Los Angeles in 2010 to pursue a career in filmmaking.
Started out at a camera rental house, networked and did my fair share of grinding, and became a freelance camera assistant.
After joining I.A.T.S.E. Local 600 (International Cinematographers Guild), my career and opportunities advanced progressively.
For a solid 8 years I worked as a 1st A.C., also known as a Focus Puller, and eventually worked my way up to Camera Operator.
I was fortunate enough to work on a variety of commercials, feature films, television shows, and an academy award winning documentary.
I had to make a tough decision to gracefully bow out of the industry to focus on my family.
It's an exciting industry but lacks sustainability, predictability, and is almost impossible to find a suitable work-life balance.
I'll always have a sweet spot in my heart for the filmmaking process!
