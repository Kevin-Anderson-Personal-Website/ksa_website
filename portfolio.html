Tremors movies I've watched, in no particular order

Tremors

The first of the franchise and my personal favorite.
The monsters are sand worms in this one.

Tremors 2

The second of the franchise and another great film.
The monsters are these huge bugs that work together very well.

Tremors 3

The third of the franchise and I have to admit I haven't seen it.
Michael Gross is in it so that adds some value.
The monsters look like sand worms again only much angrier.
